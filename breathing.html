Box Breathing (4-4-4-4)

Inhale 4s → hold 4s → exhale 4s → hold 4s. Creates a steady rhythm that interrupts the stress response and restores clarity.

😴

4-7-8 Breathing

Inhale 4s → hold 7s → exhale 8s. The long exhale signals safety to your nervous system — very effective before sleep.

💆

Equal Breathing (5-5)

Inhale 5s → exhale 5s. Symmetric rhythm creates mental steadiness. A great first technique if you're new to breathwork.

💡

Why it works

Slow breathing activates your vagus nerve, reducing heart rate and cortisol. Regular daily practice over 2–4 weeks measurably improves baseline anxiety.
